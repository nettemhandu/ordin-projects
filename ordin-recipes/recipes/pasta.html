Creamy Pasta

Home

Description

This creamy, garlicky pasta is ready in less than 15 minutes and SO easy to throw together! If you want quick comfort food, look no further!

Ingredients

  • pasta (spaghetti, penne, fettuccine, etc.)
  • 2 tablespoons butter or olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream or whole milk
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • fresh parsley, chopped (optional, for garnish)
  • chicken breast, bacon, or other meat

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter or heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Sprinkle the flour over the garlic and stir well to combine. Cook for about 1-2 minutes to remove the raw flour taste.
  4. Gradually whisk in the chicken or vegetable broth, followed by the heavy cream or whole milk. Continue to whisk until the mixture is smooth and starts to thicken, about 3-5 minutes.
  5. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until melted and the sauce is creamy. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to coat the pasta evenly with the sauce. If using, add cooked chicken breast, bacon, or other meat at this stage.
  7. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes to heat everything through.
  8. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley if desired, and serve immediately.
