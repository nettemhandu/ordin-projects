Cottage Pie

Home

Description

Cottage pie is a traditional British dish made with ground meat, typically beef or lamb, cooked in a savory gravy and topped with a layer of creamy mashed potatoes. The dish isbaked in the oven until the mashed potatoes are golden brown and crispy on top. Cottage pie is a comforting and hearty meal that is perfect for cold weather and is often served with vegetables such as peas, carrots, or green beans.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef or lamb
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4 cups mashed potatoes (prepared with butter and milk)
  • 2 tablespoons butter (for topping)
  • 1/4 cup grated cheddar cheese (optional, for topping)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef or lamb over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  3. Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, and diced carrots to the skillet. Cook until the vegetables are softened, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Stir in the tomato paste, beef broth, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook for about 10 minutes until the sauce has thickened. Stir in the frozen peas and cook for an additional 2 minutes.
  5. Transfer the meat mixture to a baking dish and spread it out evenly.
  6. Spread the mashed potatoes over the meat mixture, smoothing it out with a spatula. Dot the top with butter and sprinkle with grated cheddar cheese if using.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes, or until the mashed potatoes are golden brown and crispy on top.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley if desired.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious cottage pie!
